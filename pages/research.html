EU Consolidated Tape reconstruction

EuroCTP, Amsterdam · Jun–Aug 2025 · Internship

Context. MiFID II fragmented European equity trading across dozens of venues, destroying the consolidated view of best prices that a centralised exchange would provide. MiFIR Article 22a mandates a Consolidated Tape Provider (CTP) for equities; on 19 December 2025, ESMA selected EuroCTP as the first equities CTP for the EU, with a target go-live in July 2026.

Work. Exploratory microstructure research on BMLL Level 3 order book data to inform the design of the equity consolidated tape. Mapped market events and microstructure biases capable of distorting the EBBO (European Best Bid and Offer), auction transitions, crossed/locked books, stale quotes, venue-level latency skew and proposed data-quality checks and acceptance thresholds to filter them. Studied the statistical power of candidate EBBO estimators and stress-tested the tape's reconstruction algorithm against these edge cases.

Tools. Pandas, PySpark, SQL, Clustering. Data: BMLL Data Lab.
